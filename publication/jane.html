Title: "Pride and Prejudice"
Author: Jane Austen
Publication Date: 1813

Publication Details:
"Pride and Prejudice" is a renowned novel written by Jane Austen. It was published in 1813 and has since become one of the most beloved works of English literature. The novel tells the story of Elizabeth Bennet, a witty and independent young woman, and her tumultuous relationship with the proud Mr. Darcy. Set in the English countryside during the Regency era, "Pride and Prejudice" explores themes of love, marriage, social class, and the role of women in society. Austen's sharp wit, memorable characters, and insightful commentary on societal norms make this novel a timeless classic.
